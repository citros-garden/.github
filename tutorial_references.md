3. [Dummy Robot](https://docs.ros.org/en/foxy/Tutorials/Demos/dummy-robot-demo.html)
4. [Foxglove Blog - Parameters](https://foxglove.dev/blog/how-to-use-ros2-parameters)
5. [PX4 Simulation](https://docs.px4.io/main/en/sim_gazebo_classic/)
6. [Turtlebot3](https://emanual.robotis.com/docs/en/platform/turtlebot3/simulation/#gazebo-simulation)
7. [Nav2 SLAM](https://navigation.ros.org/tutorials/docs/navigation2_with_slam.html)
